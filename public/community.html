Community & Culture

Where Belonging Meets Tradition

The Yard Pass is more than a savings card — it's a celebration of who we are. From homecoming weekends and step shows to chapter meetings and family reunions, we show up together, save together, and proudly represent the HBCU legacy that shapes us every day.

Our Core Values

Pride

Your HBCU experience isn't just a memory — it's a living identity. The Yard Pass is built for those who wear their letters, wave their colors, and carry their school's legacy everywhere they go. Every discount you unlock is a reminder that your community has your back, on and off the yard.

Belonging

From homecomings to step shows, chapter meetings to leadership summits — the HBCU world is built on showing up together. Yard Pass membership connects you to a nationwide network of alumni, Greeks, and culture-keepers who share your story, your traditions, and your drive to thrive.

Empowerment

Financial empowerment is cultural empowerment. With access to over one million discounts across dining, travel, entertainment, and everyday services, the Yard Pass transforms every purchase into an opportunity to keep more money in your pocket — and more power in your community.
