Got Questions?

Everything You Need to Know About Yard Pass

We've answered the most common questions from our community. If you need more, we're always just a message away.

Who is Yard Pass designed for?

Yard Pass was built specifically for the HBCU community — students, alumni, Greek-letter organization members, faculty, and anyone who proudly represents the HBCU lifestyle. Whether you're attending homecoming, a step show, a leadership summit, or simply going about your everyday life, Yard Pass is your trusted companion for saving money while representing the organizations and traditions you love.

01

What types of savings and discounts are included?

Your Yard Pass membership unlocks access to over one million discounts across the country through a massive nationwide savings network. Savings span everyday categories including dining, travel, shopping, entertainment, health and wellness services, and vacation planning. Every time you dine out, book a trip, shop for essentials, or plan a family outing, your Yard Pass transforms that everyday spending into extraordinary savings opportunities — including exclusive deals aligned with the HBCU lifestyle like cruises and cultural events.

02

How does my membership support everyday use?

Yard Pass is designed to be a card you reach for every single day — not just on special occasions. Use it at restaurants, retail stores, gyms like Anytime Fitness for membership discounts, service providers, and travel platforms. The savings network is built for real life, meaning whether you're grabbing lunch between classes, booking a weekend getaway, or shopping for your chapter, your membership is actively working for you. It's the kind of everyday savings that quietly adds up to something meaningful over time.

03

Can organizations inquire about group membership options?

Absolutely. Yard Pass was created with community at its core, and we welcome inquiries from Greek-letter organizations, alumni associations, student government bodies, HBCU chapters, and other community groups interested in group or bulk membership arrangements. Whether you're planning to offer Yard Pass as a member benefit at chapter meetings, conferences, or homecoming events, we'd love to connect and explore what makes sense for your organization. Reach out through our contact form and a member of our team will follow up with you directly.
